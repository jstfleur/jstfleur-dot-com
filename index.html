About

Who is this guy?

My name is Jovencey StFleur and I am an aspiring UX designer, currently based in sunny South Florida. I was a fifth year Computer Engineering major at the University of Florida, minoring in Digital Arts and Sciences, before taking an academic leave.

Coming from a background in both engineering and design, I love connecting the dots between business goals, accessibility and user satisfaction. In my free time, I enjoy playing video games, binging reality TV, and planning my future trip around the world.

I'm actively seeking junior UX design positions for the near future and I hope to work with you! If you like my work, please don't hesitate to reach out at hi@jstfleur.com or LinkedIn.
